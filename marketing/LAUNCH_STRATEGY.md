Growth is engineered into the product, not just bolted on with marketing.

1.  **The "Send a Vibe" Loop:**
    *   **Action:** User A sends a vibe to User B.
    *   **Trigger:** User B receives a push notification: "[User A] just sent you good vibes! ‚ú®"
    *   **Hook:** Curiosity and social validation pull User B back into the app to see the message and their new vibration count.

2.  **The "Photo Tag" Loop:**
    *   **Action:** A user uploads a photo of a location and tags a friend they were with.
    *   **Trigger:** The friend gets a notification: "You were tagged in a photo at [Location Name]!"
    *   **Hook:** This drives social sharing and brings new users into the app through personal connection.

3.  **The "Vibe Score Share" Loop:**
    *   **Action:** After a measurement, the user is presented with a beautifully designed, shareable graphic of their result.
    *   **Trigger:** A one-tap button to share to Instagram Stories.
    *   **Hook:** The graphic is designed to be intriguing and slightly mysterious, prompting followers to ask, "What is this?" and driving curiosity-led downloads.
