## üí¨ Brand Voice

### Voice Characteristics
- **Tone**: Confident, curious, slightly mystical
- **Language**: Mix of scientific and spiritual terminology
- **Style**: Short, punchy, visual

- Overuse emoji (keep it minimal, intentional)

## üé® Brand Guidelines Summary
